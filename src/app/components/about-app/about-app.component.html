Gal's Coupons

This is a coupon system demo app.
You can sign in as an Administrator (Admin), Company, or Customer.

The Admin can:

  • See all companies' and all customers' details.
  • Update their details.
  • Add a new company or a new customer.
  • Delete any company or customer.

Companies can:

  • See and update their profile.
  • See all their coupons.
  • Update any coupon.
  • Create new coupons.
  • Delete any coupon.

Customers can:

  • See and update their profile.
  • See the coupons they own.
  • See all available coupons.
  • Buy new coupons.

Registration

  1. There are two ways to register as a new company or a new customer:
    • The admin can add them with their details.
    • They can sign up and register their details.
    • If you'd like to register as an admin to check out the interface, please contact us via email or whatsapp.
  2. A new company cannot register with a name or an email that is used by another company.
  3. A new customer cannot register with an email that is used by another customer.

Restrictions

  1. It is NOT possible to update:
    • The ID of a company, a customer or a coupon.
    • The name of a company.
  2. When creating a coupon, the company cannot use a title that is already used for one of its coupons.
  3. Each coupon can be purchased only once by each customer.

On Deletion

  1. When a coupon is deleted by a company:
    • Customers who own this coupon would no longer see this coupon in their list.
    • All other customers would no longer see this coupon as available for purchase.
  2. When a company is deleted by the admin, all its coupons are automatically deleted.

Coupon's expiration

Each coupon has an expiration date. When a coupon expires it is automatically deleted from the system, with the same consequences as when a coupon is deleted by a company.

Out of stock

When a coupon is out of stock:

  • The coupon would still appear in its company's list, with an amount of a zero.
  • It would appear in the coupon's list of the customers who purchased it.
  • But it wouldn't appear in the available coupons list for customers who don't own it.
